ACTION REQUIRED: Termination of Energy Credits

Explanation

The OBBBA systematically repeals the clean energy tax credits expanded by the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA). This represents a significant policy shift away from incentivizing green energy. Clients who made investment decisions based on the availability of these credits need to be notified immediately, as their expected return on investment may be severely impacted.
